How to use

Fill in the customer context → generate a clean demo narrative.

This is a reusable structure: discovery recap, problem→feature mapping, value/ROI framing, and a crisp close. It’s meant to feel “consultative” instead of “feature tour.”

2) Output

This is the talk track you can use live, or paste into an email recap. Keep it crisp.

Demo narrative
Checklist
  • • Confirm stakeholders + roles
  • • Recap pains in their language
  • • Show 3–5 flows, not 30 features
  • • Tie every click to a metric
  • • Close with a low-risk next step
Closing line

“If this matches what you need, the next step is a short technical validation with your team so we can confirm integration, data flows, and timeline. Sound fair?”
